## About the Main Presentation

The resident-facing slide deck is written for people who swim, fish, and share stewardship of Chrysler Pond. No prior knowledge of data science or remote sensing is assumed. The focus is on what the data does and does not tell us about whether the EMF device has reduced harmful algal blooms.

Key messages:
- Bloom activity at Chrysler Pond is highly variable from year to year
- The satellite record does not show consistent suppression following device deployment
- Results are inconclusive — this is not a final verdict, but a call for longer-term, more rigorous monitoring
